## A-DAP positioning

A-DAP does not claim originality in these primitives.

The intended contribution is the composition of:

- pre-action decision binding;
- conservative custody-domain collapse;
- temporal custody graphs;
- NDC as a structural falsification-cost metric;
- detectability-boundary mapping for opaque AI decision systems.
