# PL2338 Scope Lock

## Included

- Contestability
- Accountability
- Human oversight
- Recordkeeping
- Reconstruction capability

## Excluded

- Fairness
- Bias
- Constitutional interpretation
- Economic impact
- Regulatory desirability

## Unit of Analysis

The unit of analysis is not legal validity.

The unit of analysis is the existence of independent verification paths after dependency collapse.

## Primary Question

What theoretical NDC is implied by the oversight and contestability structure described in PL 2338/2023?
