# Contributing to A-DAP

Thank you for your interest in reviewing, challenging, or improving A-DAP.

A-DAP — Auditable Decision Accountability Protocol — is a verifiability architecture for externally reconstructible automated decisions.

This repository welcomes structured contributions, especially:

- conceptual criticism;
- reconstruction attempts;
- adversarial attacks;
- proof-boundary analysis;
- documentation improvements;
- verification workflow improvements;
- solver-related proposals.

A-DAP is strengthened by serious criticism.

The goal is not to protect claims from challenge.

The goal is to make every claim more precise, reconstructible, or falsifiable.
